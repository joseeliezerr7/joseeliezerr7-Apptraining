================================================================
TECH ADVANCEMENT — IARC Content Rating questionnaire (DRAFT)
================================================================

Open Play Console → Policy → App content → Content rating →
"Start questionnaire". Email defaults to the developer account
email — keep it.

Recommended app category (asked first):
  ► EDUCATION
  (Tools/Reference also acceptable; Education matches the
   onboarding copy: "Train at your pace", "Manuals at hand".)

Once the category is set, IARC presents a category-specific list
of yes/no questions. For this app the answer is "No" to every
one of them. Cross-reference table below — if any of these is
actually true in your real training catalog, flip the answer.

----------------------------------------------------------------
1) VIOLENCE
----------------------------------------------------------------
[ NO ] Does the app contain violence?
[ NO ] Does the app contain realistic violence (acts of violence
       that look real)?
[ NO ] Does the app contain blood or gore?
[ NO ] Does the app contain violence against vulnerable groups
       or identifiable real groups?
[ NO ] Does the app contain violence with weapons?

⚠️  If any training video shows industrial/workplace injuries,
   first aid demos with realistic blood, defensive-tactics
   footage, or similar — flip the relevant answer.

----------------------------------------------------------------
2) SEXUAL CONTENT
----------------------------------------------------------------
[ NO ] Does the app contain sexual content, nudity, sexual
       innuendo, or references to sex?
[ NO ] Does the app contain explicit nudity, sexual acts, or
       sexual content involving minors?

----------------------------------------------------------------
3) PROFANITY & CRUDE HUMOR
----------------------------------------------------------------
[ NO ] Does the app contain profanity or crude humor?
[ NO ] Does the app contain strong/explicit profanity or crude
       references involving sexual or bodily functions?

⚠️  If your training videos include occasional informal
   language ("damn", "hell"), the conservative answer is still
   "No". Only flip if there is regular strong profanity.

----------------------------------------------------------------
4) CONTROLLED SUBSTANCES
----------------------------------------------------------------
[ NO ] Does the app contain references to or depict use of
       alcohol, tobacco, or drugs?
[ NO ] Does the app contain instructions on how to make/use
       drugs, alcohol, or tobacco?

----------------------------------------------------------------
5) GAMBLING
----------------------------------------------------------------
[ NO ] Does the app contain simulated gambling (no real money)?
[ NO ] Does the app enable real-money gambling?

----------------------------------------------------------------
6) MISCELLANEOUS / SENSITIVE THEMES
----------------------------------------------------------------
[ NO ] Does the app contain content that may be disturbing to
       some users (horror themes, jump scares, etc.)?
[ NO ] Does the app contain references to discrimination based
       on race, gender, religion, sexuality, etc.?
[ NO ] Does the app contain content that promotes/glorifies
       self-harm, suicide, eating disorders?
[ NO ] Does the app contain content that promotes terrorism or
       extremist organizations?

----------------------------------------------------------------
7) INTERACTIVE ELEMENTS (these are NOT about the rating itself,
   but Play uses them to surface advisories like "users interact")
----------------------------------------------------------------
[ NO ] Do users interact with each other inside the app?
       (no chat, no comments, no DMs, no forum, no social feed)
[ NO ] Does the app share the user's location with other users?
[ NO ] Does the app share personal information with other users?
[ NO ] Does the app enable purchases of digital goods?
       (no IAP in current build)
[ NO ] Does the app give users unrestricted access to the
       Internet (open web browser)?
       (we only fetch from our own Supabase + open in-app
        video player + PDF viewer — no general web browsing)
[ NO ] Does the app contain user-generated content shared with
       others? (notes & bookmarks are private to each account)

----------------------------------------------------------------
EXPECTED RESULTING RATINGS
----------------------------------------------------------------
With all "No" answers + Education category, expect:

  IARC Generic    : 3+
  ESRB (US)       : Everyone

  ClassInd (BR)   : L  (Livre)
