[EN]
Tech Advancement is the all-in-one training app for teams and professionals who want to learn at their own pace. Watch high-quality training videos, follow complete programs, and access a full library of PDF manuals — all from your phone, tablet, or browser.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

• Watch training videos organized by category
• Follow complete programs that walk you through a topic, step by step
• Browse and search PDF manuals in English and Spanish
• Take notes on any video and review them later
• Bookmark your favorite videos and manuals
• Download videos and manuals for offline use — perfect for travel or low-connectivity days
• Pick up where you left off across phone, tablet, and web

BUILT FOR REAL LEARNING

• Bilingual content (English / Spanish) with a one-tap language switch
• Continuous progress tracking — never lose your place
• Featured highlights so you always know where to start
• Clean, focused interface designed for distraction-free learning

OFFLINE-FIRST

Save videos and manuals to your device and keep learning even without a signal. Perfect for commuting, traveling, or working from sites with limited connectivity.

PRIVACY & ACCOUNT CONTROL

Your data stays yours. You can update your profile, change your password, or delete your account and all associated data at any time, directly from the app.
