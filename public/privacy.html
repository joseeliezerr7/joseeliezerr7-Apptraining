We collect the data you provide at sign-up (name, email, country, optional phone), authentication metadata, and basic usage signals such as which videos and manuals you open.

We use your data to authenticate you, personalize your experience, recommend training content and respond to support requests.

Data is stored with our backend provider (Supabase) under industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Sessions on mobile devices are stored in secure platform storage.

We do not sell your data. We share it only with processors necessary to run the service (authentication, hosting, storage).

You can edit your profile from the app, change your password, or delete your account permanently. Contact us to request a data export. To request account deletion outside the app, see delete-account.

This service is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has registered, contact us to have the account removed.
